## Fundamental Analysis

### Financial Strength
FY2025 revenue was $16.96B (+31% YoY), operating cash flow $7.69B (+71%), and free cash flow $3.87B (+194%). Barrick ended Q4 with $6.71B cash and $4.70B debt (net cash ~$2.0B). FY2025 production was 3.26Moz gold and 220kt copper, both in line with guidance.

### Competitive Position
Barrick remains a scale gold/copper producer with meaningful reserve life: proven and probable gold reserves of 85Moz and copper reserves of 18Mt (attributable). Its asset mix plus North America IPO pathway gives a credible value-unlock angle versus pure-play peers.

### Management & Governance
Management delivered FY2025 guidance, raised the base dividend, repurchased $1.5B of stock, and introduced a 50% attributable FCF payout framework. Capital allocation has been disciplined; key execution risk now is IPO timing and delivery against 2026 plan.
